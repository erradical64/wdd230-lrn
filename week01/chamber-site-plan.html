Our founding and what do we do?

We are a chamber of commerce located and founded in 2023 in Providence, Cache County of Utah. The Providence Chamber of Commerce acts as a economic catalyst, as well as leadership convener and community champion in the city of Providence and its neighboring cities. We promote business through member services, economic and community development, and public policy that balances economic prosperity.
